# SQL Projects

## Overview

This repository contains SQL projects that demonstrate my ability to solve real-world business problems using MySQL. Each project focuses on analyzing large datasets, writing optimized SQL queries, and applying analytical techniques to generate actionable insights.

Across these projects, I use SQL to perform data exploration, trend analysis, KPI reporting, ranking, segmentation, and business analysis while leveraging advanced SQL concepts.

---

## Projects

### 1. Credit Card Transaction Analysis

**Overview**

Analyzed credit card transaction data to answer business and analytical questions related to customer spending behavior, city performance, card usage, and transaction trends.

**Dataset**

The dataset contains credit card transaction records across multiple Indian cities, including transaction dates, card types, expense categories, customer demographics, and transaction amounts.

**Key Business Questions**

* Top 5 cities by total spend
* Highest spending month by card type
* Cumulative spending milestone analysis
* Gold card spend percentage
* Highest and lowest expense type by city
* Female spend contribution analysis
* Month-over-month growth analysis
* Weekend spending behavior
* Time taken for cities to reach their 500th transaction

---

### 2. Olympic History Analysis

**Overview**

Analyzed historical Olympic athlete and event data to answer questions related to medal performance, athlete achievements, country success, and Olympic trends.

**Dataset**

The dataset contains Olympic athlete participation records, countries represented, sporting events, medals won, Olympic years, and seasons.

**Key Business Questions**

* Team with the highest number of gold medals
* Total silver medals by team and year of maximum silver medals
* Athlete with the most gold medals among athletes who only won gold
* Top gold medal winners for each Olympic year
* India's first Gold, Silver, and Bronze medals
* Athletes who won gold medals in both Summer and Winter Olympics
* Athletes who won Gold, Silver, and Bronze in a single Olympic Games
* Athletes who won gold medals in the same event across three consecutive Summer Olympics since 2000
